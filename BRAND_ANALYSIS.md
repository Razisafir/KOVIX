> **Product**: Desktop AI coding agent with persistent memory, multi-agent system, and autonomous execution

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The name **"Construct"** faces significant challenges: construct.ai is taken by a construction-industry AI company, construct.dev redirects to AWS's CDK Construct Hub, and the word is generic in both software (constructors, class constructors) and physical construction contexts. However, the brand has been partially established with "Construct AI" copyright and theme naming ("construct-dark"). This analysis evaluates the current name against 8 criteria and presents 20 rigorously researched alternatives.

### Verdict on "Construct"
The name is serviceable but deeply flawed. The SEO competition is insurmountable, domain availability is poor, and trademark protection would be weak. The product's key differentiators (persistent memory, 24/7 background operation, multi-agent system) are not reflected in the name at all. A rebrand is recommended before significant marketing investment.

## TOP 5 RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. **MACHINA** — Primary Recommendation

**Why**: Highest composite score. Distinctive without being obscure. Latin roots convey sophistication and permanence — fitting for a professional Bloomberg-terminal-aesthetic product. The "machine intelligence" connotation perfectly captures an autonomous coding agent. Excellent domain availability (machina.dev, machina.app). Near-zero trademark conflicts in software. Strong SEO potential. Memorable after one exposure.

**Brand construction**: Machina (primary), "Machina Agent" (full product name), machina.dev (primary domain). Theme: "machina-dark." The Latin pluralization (Machinae) could name the multi-agent system.

### 2. **KERN** — The Ultra-Short Contender

**Why**: Four characters — as short as "grep" or "npm." Implies the product is the kernel/core of development. Hard-hitting, terminal-native sound. Bloomberg-terminal aesthetic match. German/Dutch root for "seed/origin" adds depth. Massive developer appeal (kernel = OS core = developer religion).

**Risk**: Minimalism can feel insubstantial. Needs strong visual branding to carry weight.

**Brand construction**: kern (command-line style, lowercase), kern.dev, "kern agent" (full name).

### 3. **AXIOM** — The Intellectual Choice
